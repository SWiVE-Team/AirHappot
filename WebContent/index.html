Why we born：

Due to the disability of matching supply and demand in time, AirHappot can improve the efficiency of matching public spare materials and volunteer resources with charity needs by achieving higher visibility of material idle status and future needs. It can also be used for pre-disaster prevention purpose, if needed.

How we do：

AirHappot provides an online partitioned virtual space for every individual. Each depot can be further divided into several virtual areas depending on the purpose. Providers, whom we call Depoters, can manage the information of spare items and publish the information to those who might need them now or later in the future. Charity groups will be facilitated by AirHappot for optimized supply and demand matchmaking mechanism considering logistic distance. By analyzing the idle status of spare resources of the platform, visibility improvement will directly contributes to better preparation for predicted or unpredicted needs in the future. Moreover, we can also ally external resources for better handling long term idle materials. AirHappot also support large-scale data collection and online analysis to reveal the utilization and distribution performances of the materials.

WITH AIRHAPPOT, YOU CAN HAVE THE EASIEST WAY TO ENJOY SHARING!


Due to the disability of matching supply and demand in time, AirHappot can improve the efficiency of matching public spare goods and volunteer resources with charity needs by achieving higher visibility of material idle status and future needs. It can also be used for pre-disaster prevention purpose, if needed.


AirHappot provides an online partitioned virtual space for every individual. Each depot can be further divided into several virtual areas. Providers, whom we call Depoters, can manage spare goods and publish to potential Demanders. Charity groups will be facilitated by AirHappot for optimized supply and demand matchmaking mechanism considering logistic distance. By analyzing the utilization of spare resources, visibility improvement will directly contributes to better preparation for future needs. Moreover, we can also ally external resources for better minimize long term wastage. AirHappot also supports large-scale data collection and online analysis to reveal the utilization and distribution performances of products.
